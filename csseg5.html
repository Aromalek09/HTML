THAR ROXX(4*4)

REVIEW

"The Thar Roxx boasts a new six-slat grille, 18-inch alloy wheels, and a standard black roof across all variants ¹. Its interior is more spacious, with better shoulder and knee room in the second row, making it ideal for family road trips [4). This SUV is built for adventure, featuring a Penta Link suspension system, 41.7-degree approach angle, 36.1-degree departure angle, and 650mm water-wading depth ¹. The 4x4 drivetrain is available with the diesel engine, making it a beast on off-road terrain. The Thar Roxx offers a range of engine options, including a 2.2-liter diesel engine producing 150bhp/330Nm and a 2.0-liter turbo petrol engine producing 174bhp/380Nm ². The diesel engine is smooth and exciting to drive, with a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. The Thar Roxx starts at Rs. 12.99 lakh for the base model, going up to Rs. 22.49 lakh for the top-end variant [4).
